About Us

Hello and welcome to our website GroovedIn! We at GroovedIn are a small close-knit team of dedicated web developers who eat, sleep, and breathe all things music and web-related. Our journey began with a shared idea: to revolutionize the way individuals receive their news about all things music, and become one of the biggest and most trustworthy music entertainment sites out here. With creativity in our hearts we are committed to turning visions into reality.

Our Story

GroovedIn was born from a shared passion for music and an unwavering belief in the power of the web.
